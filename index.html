About Marilyn

I'm Marilyn, a third-year computer science student at UC Berkeley. I started in economics my freshman year and quickly decided to go the computer science route due to wanting to challenge myself with a versatile degree. On the software engineering side, I interned at Atlassian last summer on their Bitbucket Cloud team and I'll be at Meta this upcoming summer, hopefully working on some VR stuff with MetaQuest. I've been super interested in computer graphics and rendering lately.


On the non-CS side, I am an actress actively taking theater and film acting classes on the side, as well as working with directors on short films. Being on camera, with either acting, vlogging, or game streaming, is my greatest joy. With acting, making the audience feel an emotion they weren't able to process previously is awesome. As a musician who has been playing the piano and flute for 15 years now, I find acting very refreshing: it is using my body and face as instruments to allow people to feel something. I'm interested in film acting more than theater.


On the other other side, I enjoy playing and streaming League of Legends with friends, going to NBA games (Warriors specifically), and running, weightlifting, and playing basketball and tennis. I would like to run in the next SF marathon.
