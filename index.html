About Marilyn

I'm Marilyn, a third-year computer science student at UC Berkeley. I started in economics my freshman year and quickly decided to go the computer science route due to wanting to challenge myself with a versatile degree. I interned at Atlassian last summer on their Bitbucket Cloud team doing backend, and I'm currently interning at Meta, working on their backend server. Computer graphics has always been really cool to me, since I'm a mobile and PC player.


Non CS-wise, I am an actress working with both professional & unprofessional filmmakers and directors, filming short films on the side. Being on camera via acting, vlogging, or Twitch streaming, has always felt so natural to me. With acting, I want to allow the audience to feel that "shock" factor of an emotion they may not have been able to previously process. My all-time favorite film is Interstellar, and current favorite film is Challengers.


Lastly, I love watching NBA, League, playing tennis, and running/lifting.
